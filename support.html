frequently asked questions

how do i cancel my subscription?

subscriptions are managed through your apple id. to cancel:

  1. open the settings app on your iphone
  2. tap your name at the top
  3. tap "subscriptions"
  4. tap "sera"
  5. tap "cancel subscription"

you'll keep access until the end of your current billing period.

how do i restore my purchase?

if you bought sera premium or a subscription on another device:

  1. open sera
  2. tap the settings icon (top right)
  3. select "restore purchases"
  4. your purchase will be restored automatically

make sure you're signed in with the same apple id you used to purchase.

what payment methods do you accept?

all purchases go through apple's app store, so you can use any payment method linked to your apple id — credit cards, debit cards, apple pay, or app store credit.

how do i delete my account or data?

sera doesn't create accounts — all your photos stay on your device and in your icloud. to delete everything:

  1. delete any photos you took with sera from your photo library
  2. delete the sera app from your iphone

that's it. we don't store your photos on our servers.

my photos aren't saving. what should i do?

make sure sera has permission to access your photo library:

  1. open the settings app
  2. scroll down and tap "sera"
  3. tap "photos"
  4. select "all photos" or "add photos only"
can i use sera on ipad or android?

sera is currently only available for iphone. we designed it specifically for the iphone camera experience. no plans for ipad or android right now, but never say never!

what ios version do i need?

sera works on iphone running ios 15 or later. we recommend keeping your phone updated to the latest ios version for the best experience.

how do i report a bug or request a feature?

found a bug? want to suggest a feature? email us at support@sera.camera with:

  • what happened (for bugs) or what you'd like to see (for features)
  • your iphone model and ios version
  • screenshots if relevant

we read every message and genuinely appreciate your feedback.
